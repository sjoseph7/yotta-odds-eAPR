More Information

Yotta, presumably named after the largest decimal unit prefix in the metric system, is a prize-linked savings account that gamifies saving money. In addition to offering a traditional interest rate, Yotta gives savers weekly opportunities to win cash prizes. These prizes can be treated as interest on the balance in the account, but due to the random nature of lotteries it can be difficult to estimate the account's APR, or annual percentage rate (of return). This calculator estimates the effective APR ("eAPR") using the Yotta's publicly available odds of winning (see official rules) and can also estimate a "nominal" eAPR based on the number of tickets issued.
